What is Looloop

A digital habit companion
for a physical journal

Looloop is the app layer of a premium 90-day habit and sleep tracker journal sold by Doo Doodle. While the journal is the tactile, analog experience — the Looloop app is its living, breathing digital twin. Together they form a phygital loop: the journal grounds you in the present, the app reflects your journey over time.

No other Indian journal brand has a companion app. This is Looloop's core competitive moat — built to deepen engagement, reduce churn, and add a premium digital layer that justifies a higher price point at scale.

Core Features

Everything in one loop

Built for the full 90-day journey — from day one setup to the completion certificate.

🎯

Daily Habit Log

Log up to 10 custom habits per day — fully blank, named by the user. No presets, no judgement. Tap to mark done. The app tracks streaks, perfect days, and monthly completion automatically. Every logged day feeds the radial fan chart.

🌙

Sleep Log

Log bedtime, wake time, and a 1–5 quality rating each day. The app calculates a 0–100 Sleep Score across three dimensions — duration (40 pts), quality (40 pts), and consistency (20 pts) — giving users weekly insight into their rest patterns.

🌈

Mood Tracker

A radial sunburst mood tracker with the character at its centre. 30 daily segments, colour-coded by mood rating. Visualises emotional patterns across the full month in a single glance.

📊

Monthly Summary

The signature radial fan chart shows the entire month at a glance — every day × every habit rendered as a beveled 3-D tile with ambient glow. Tabs switch between Habits, Sleep, and Mood views. Tap any tile to log or edit that day.

📸

Journal Scan (AI Vision)

Point your camera at any page of the physical journal. Claude AI reads the habit grid and auto-counts ticks. Images are compressed client-side before sending. Free users get 1 scan/week; premium gets up to 3/day.

🤖

AI Character Dialogue

Each journal edition ships with an AI character who speaks in-character based on the user's current streak. Powered by Claude Haiku — responses are one line, never preachy, always on-brand. Free users: 10 messages/day.

Today Dashboard

Your day, at a glance

The home screen shows today's progress through Apple Watch-style concentric activity rings — one for habits, one for sleep. Below the rings: metric cards, a 7-day week strip, and the AI character chat bubble.

Activity Rings — outer ring tracks habit completion (done/total), inner ring tracks sleep vs. 8-hour goal. Animated, colour-coded by character palette.

Week Strip — 7-day scrollable strip at the top showing each day's habit and sleep rings in miniature. Tap any day to jump to that log.

Streak Counter — live habit streak displayed with a flame icon. Streak is active if the user logged habits today or yesterday.

Character Chat — the AI character responds in-context to the user's streak. One-tap to open, one-line responses, streak-aware, free limit of 10/day.

Premium / Certificate links — quick access to the Upgrade page or, for premium users, confirmation badge. Completion certificate link always visible.

Signature Visualisations

Data made beautiful

Every chart in Looloop is custom-built in SVG — no third-party chart libraries. The visual language is inspired by the journal's physical grid layout, brought to life with glow, gradients, and depth.

🌀

Radial Fan Chart

Month × habit grid rendered as a leftward fan. Each tile is a beveled 3-D chip with ambient particle glow. Bullet-journal guide lines label each habit ring. Tap any tile to edit that day.

🌊

Sleep Garden

A stacked terracotta-pot visual mirroring the physical journal tracker. Sleep duration fills the pot layers. Mirrors the analog experience digitally.

☀️

Mood Sunburst

Radial 30-segment mood ring with the character emoji at centre. Colour intensity scales with mood rating. Full month in one circle.

💤

Sleep Score Card

Weekly 0–100 score across duration (40pt), quality (40pt), consistency (20pt). Shareable as an image card. Premium feature.

🏆

90-Day Certificate

Completion certificate generated at day 90. LinkedIn-shareable. Branded with the user's character and final stats. Premium feature.

📅

Weekly View

7-day grid for habits + sleep timeline bar chart. Compact, navigable by week. Habit completion pills + sleep stat pills.

Tech Stack

Built lean.
Designed to scale.

The entire stack runs on free tiers in Phase 1 — with clear upgrade paths as usage grows. AI costs are kept ultra-low by using Claude Haiku exclusively and enforcing per-user daily limits in the database.

Layer Technology Notes
Web frontend Next.js 14 (App Router) Hosted on Vercel free tier. TypeScript throughout.
Mobile (Month 6) React Native + Expo iOS + Android from one codebase. Converts from web.
Database + Auth Supabase Magic link auth. Row-level security on every table. Free tier.
AI Dialogue Claude Haiku 4.5 Character responses. ~₹0.02/message. Max 10/user/day (free).
AI Vision Claude Haiku 4.5 Journal grid scan. ~₹0.10/scan. Max 3/user/day.
Payments Razorpay ₹99/month subscription. 1.9% per transaction. India-first.
Notifications Expo Notifications 9pm daily, character-voiced. Free.
Image sharing html2canvas / Satori Sleep score cards, completion certificates.
